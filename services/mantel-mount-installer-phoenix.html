The Problem

Most installers won't touch a pull-down mount.

MantelMount and similar gas-piston pull-down systems can weigh 60-90 lbs before you add the TV. They need real stud-anchored support, often a horizontal mounting board to bridge studs at the right height, and exact clearance from the mantel and ceiling. Get any of it wrong and the mount tears out of the wall or the TV hits the mantel on the way down. Big-box install services and most handymen pass on these jobs — we don't.

What's Included

Done right, every time.

01

Bridge Board When Needed

When studs aren't where the bracket mounts, we install a properly anchored horizontal bridge board so weight transfers cleanly to framing — never just drywall.

02

Clearance Math

We measure mantel-to-ceiling, calculate the down-stop position, and confirm your TV won't strike the mantel or fireplace surround in any pull-down position.

03

Counterbalance Tuned

Pull-down mounts use gas pistons or springs calibrated to your specific TV weight. We tune the tension on-site so it pulls down smoothly and stays level when locked.

04

Heat-Aware Routing

Above-fireplace installs need cabling routed away from the heat plume. We use in-wall rated cable and route around the firebox, not directly above it.

Common Questions

FAQs

Are you officially MantelMount certified?
No — we're an experienced installer, not a factory-certified one. We've installed 50+ pull-down mounts across Phoenix and know the products well, but we don't claim a certification we haven't earned. If you need a factory-certified installer specifically, ask MantelMount directly.
Can I install a MantelMount over a stone or brick fireplace?
Yes. We use proper masonry anchors (Tapcons, sleeve anchors, or epoxy anchors depending on the substrate) rated for the load. Brick and stone installs typically take 30-60 minutes longer than drywall and add to the price.
Will the TV hit my mantel?
Not if the mount is installed at the right height. We check the mount's spec sheet for required mantel clearance, measure your fireplace, and confirm the math before drilling. If your mantel is too high or too close, we'll tell you upfront before you book.
Do you supply the MantelMount, or do I buy it?
Either way. Most customers buy theirs (they're widely available on Amazon and from MantelMount directly). If you'd rather we source it, we can pick one up and add it to the invoice.
How long does the install take?
Most pull-down installs run 2-3 hours: 30-45 minutes for measuring and bridge board prep, 60-90 minutes for the mount and TV, then time for cable management and counterbalance tuning.
What if my fireplace gets too hot for the TV?
If your TV currently warms up when the fireplace runs, that's a separate issue we can solve with a heat shield or by re-routing airflow. Don't put a TV above a fireplace that runs hot — you'll cook the panel. If you're not sure, text us a photo and we'll tell you.

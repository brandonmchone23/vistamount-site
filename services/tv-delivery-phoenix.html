Common Questions

FAQs

Can you buy the TV for me?
Yes — either works. Most customers pay the store directly and we pick up the order. If you'd rather skip the store entirely, we can purchase the TV on your behalf and add the cost to your invoice.
How big of a TV can you deliver?
Up to 75″ in the box and up to 85″ out of the box on a standard delivery. Bigger than that — 90″, 98″, or multiple TVs — is no problem with a little notice; we arrange the right vehicle and quote it custom.
Can I really get it the same day?
Usually, yes. Book or text by early afternoon and we can typically pick up and deliver the same day, subject to that day's schedule. Later in the day? Ask — evening slots open up more often than you'd think.
Do you deliver without mounting?
Yes — delivery-only is totally fine, starting at a $100 minimum. That said, most customers bundle the mount since we're already there: delivery drops to the add-on rate ($79–$129 by size) and it's the fastest way to go from store shelf to watching TV.
What's your delivery area?
The full Phoenix metro: Phoenix, Scottsdale, Mesa, Chandler, Gilbert, Tempe, Glendale, Peoria, plus Paradise Valley, Arcadia, Ahwatukee, and surrounding areas.
What happens to my old TV?
Add a dismount and we'll take it off the wall, patch-prep the spot for the new one, and haul the old TV away for responsible recycling — all in the same trip.
